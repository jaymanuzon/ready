2. What this is about

The KI-ready-Check is a self-check. Your answers to the 30 statements are computed into an assessment in your browser. Only when you enter your e-mail address at the end and give your explicit consent is any data transmitted to us — so that we can send you the assessment by e-mail and (if you wish) get in touch with you.

3. What data we process

DataPurpose
Your e-mail addressSending you the assessment; contacting you, if requested
Your results profile (overall score and score per dimension)Creating and sending your assessment
Your checkboxes "Newsletter" and "Interest in the enablement programme"only if ticked: adding you to the newsletter or contacting you about the programme

Your individual answers to the 30 statements never leave your browser; only the condensed results profile is transmitted.

4. Legal basis

Processing is carried out exclusively on the basis of your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), which you give in the form before submitting. You may withdraw your consent at any time with effect for the future (e-mail to jay@aivantum.com).

5. Services used (processors)

ServiceUsed forLocation / Basis

To prevent abuse, our form endpoint (Cloudflare Worker) briefly processes your IP address on submission in order to limit the number of requests per time window (legal basis Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR). Beyond the service providers named above, your data is not shared with any other third parties.

6. Newsletter

You will only receive the newsletter if you have ticked the corresponding checkbox. Before the first mailing, you confirm your subscription via a link in a confirmation e-mail (double opt-in; set up before public release). You can unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time via the unsubscribe link or by e-mail.

7. Retention period

We delete enquiry and lead data (e-mail, results profile) once the enquiry has been handled and no further contact is requested — at the latest 24 months after the last contact. Newsletter data is stored until you unsubscribe or withdraw your consent. On request, we delete data earlier, unless statutory retention obligations require otherwise.

8. Your rights

You have the right of access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability and objection, as well as the right to withdraw your consent. To exercise these rights, contact jay@aivantum.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority; the authority responsible for us is the State Commissioner for Data Protection and Freedom of Information of North Rhine-Westphalia (LDI NRW), Kavalleriestraße 2–4, 40213 Düsseldorf, Germany.
